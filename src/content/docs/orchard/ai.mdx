Orchard ships with a built-in AI assistant: a chat panel that understands your mint and
can answer questions about it in plain language. Instead of hunting across views, you can
ask what's going on and let the assistant pull the answer together.

## What it does

- **Answer questions** — ask about balances, keysets, health, and recent activity.
- **Help you operate** — surface the right view or action for what you're trying to do.

<Aside type="note" title="Bring your own model">
  The assistant talks to a model provider you configure — that lives in
  [Settings](/orchard/settings/). Until a provider and model are set, the assistant
  stays out of your way.
</Aside>

<Aside type="caution" title="The assistant is still evolving">
  Orchard is under active development, and what the assistant can see and do is still
  expanding, so the specifics land here as they stabilize. The
  [Orchard repository](https://github.com/cashubtc/orchard) has the source code in the
  meantime.
</Aside>
