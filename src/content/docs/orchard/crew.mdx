Your crew is the set of accounts that can sign in to Orchard and operate the mint. Running
a mint is rarely a one-person job forever, and Crew lets you bring in additional operators
without handing out the account you created at [setup](/orchard/setup/).

## Members

Crew lists every account that can sign in to this Orchard instance, alongside any invites
you've sent that haven't been claimed yet. Each row shows the member, an optional **label**
to note who they are, when they were created, and a **state** — **Active** or **Inactive**
for members, **Pending** for an invite that's still open. Search and filter by state or
role to find people quickly.

## Roles

Every member holds one role that decides what they can do:

- **Admin** — full control of the instance. This is the account you created at setup; it
  can't be reassigned or removed from here.
- **Manager** — can manage the mint.
- **Reader** — can only view the application.

You invite new people as a **Manager** or **Reader**, and you can change a member's role
or switch them between Active and Inactive later. You can't edit your own account or other
admins from this page.

## Inviting people

**Invite User** opens a short form: pick a role, add an optional label, and set when the
invite **expires** (it defaults to a few hours out, or you can turn expiry off). Orchard
then generates an **invite link**, an **invite key**, and a **QR code** — share whichever
is easiest, and the new operator signs up through it to activate their account.

Until an invite is claimed it sits in the table as **Pending** with a countdown, and you
can edit it or revoke it at any time.
