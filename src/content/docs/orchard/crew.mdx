Your crew is the set of accounts that can sign in to Orchard and operate the mint. Running
a mint is rarely a one-person job forever, and Crew lets you bring in additional operators
without handing out the account you created at [setup](/orchard/setup/).

## What you'll find

- **Members** — the accounts that can sign in to this Orchard instance.
- **Access** — who can do what, so not everyone needs the keys to everything.

<Aside type="caution" title="Roles and permissions are on the way">
  Orchard is under active development, and exactly how crew members and their permissions
  are managed is still settling, so the specifics land here as they stabilize. The
  [Orchard repository](https://github.com/cashubtc/orchard) has the source code in the
  meantime.
</Aside>
