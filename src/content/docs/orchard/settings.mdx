Settings is where you tune Orchard the application — separate from the environment
variables you set when you [installed it](/install/configuration/). Those define how
Orchard connects to your services; these are the day-to-day preferences you change from
inside the dashboard.

## What you'll find

- **Application** — instance-wide preferences that apply to the whole Orchard install.
- **Device** — settings scoped to the current browser or device you're signed in from.
- **User** — your own account: profile, credentials, and personal preferences.
